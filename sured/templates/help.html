How to use [sured] the question answer platform

The idea behind [sured] is simple

  1. Anybody can ask a question.
  2. Anyone can answer.
  3. You must be logged in to either post a question or an answer
  4. When the user who asks a question is satisfied with a certain answer they select it as the answer.
  5. We encourage, religously for that matter, for users to stick to topics in professional industries and customer service.
  6. Common sense in the use of language is adviced; we feel we shouldn't have to tell you to uphold high levels of professionalism and ethics. But then again, by saying this we've just done that.
  7. The site has moderators who review content and may ban any content felt to be unethical, offensive or irrelevant to the sites purpose.
